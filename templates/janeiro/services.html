With a compassionate team of specialized healthcare professionals and family-centered care approach, we provide a nurturing environment for every stage of your child's journey. We are committed to providing exceptional care, ensuring the health, safety, and development of both mothers and children.

Theatre Services (Major & Minor)

We take pride in offering cutting-edge theatre services for both minor and major surgeries. We understand the critical role that surgical interventions play in improving the health and well-being of our patients. In-Patient & Out-Patient Services (Lab, Accident & Emergency, Pharmacy)

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare services that cater to the diverse needs of our patients. Whether you require inpatient care for more complex conditions or prefer the flexibility of outpatient services, we offer a range of high-quality and patient-centered options. With a focus on excellence, compassion, and personalized care, we strive to ensure your well-being throughout your healthcare journey.

Inpatient Services: Our inpatient services are designed to provide comprehensive care for patients requiring a higher level of medical attention, complex treatments, or surgical procedures. We offer comfortable and well-equipped rooms to ensure a restful and healing environment during your stay. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses and specialists work collaboratively to deliver personalized care, monitor your progress, and manage your treatment plan. Our inpatient services are tailored to meet your specific needs.

Outpatient Services: We understand that not all medical conditions require hospitalization, and many patients prefer the convenience of outpatient services. Our outpatient services are designed to provide timely and efficient care, allowing you to receive treatment, consultations, and diagnostic procedures without an overnight stay. Our outpatient services encompass a wide range of medical specialties, including routine check-ups, diagnostic imaging, laboratory tests, specialist consultations, minor surgeries, and counselling programs.

Imaging Services (Ultrasound & X-Ray)

We understand the critical role that diagnostic imaging plays in accurately assessing and diagnosing various medical conditions. With a commitment to utilizing cutting-edge technology, we offer exceptional ultrasound and X-ray services. Whether you require detailed imaging for precise diagnosis or routine screenings, our comprehensive diagnostic imaging services are designed to provide you with accurate results and personalized care. We offer tests on Ultrasounds, Doppler ultrasound, Abdominal ultrasound scan, Pelvic ultrasound scan, Obstetric ultrasound, Thyroid ultrasound, Testicular ultrasound scan and Prostatic ultrasound scan.

Special Clinics (General Surgery, Orthopedic Gynecology)

At our hospital, we take pride in offering a range of specialized clinics that cater to specific medical conditions and provide focused care. These clinics are staffed by expert healthcare professionals who have extensive knowledge and experience in their respective fields. Our specialized clinics are designed to address the unique needs of patients with specific conditions, ensuring that they receive comprehensive care, tailored treatment plans, and the support they require.

Orthopedic Clinic: Our orthopedic clinic focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of musculoskeletal conditions. With a team of skilled orthopedic surgeons, sports medicine specialists, and physiotherapists, we offer comprehensive services for bone and joint disorders, sports injuries, fractures, and arthritis management. Our goal is to restore mobility, relieve pain, and improve the quality of life for our patients through a combination of conservative treatments and surgical interventions when necessary.

Gynecology and Women's Health Clinic: Our gynecology and women's health clinic provides specialized care for women of all ages. Led by compassionate gynecologists and women's health specialists, we offer a wide range of services, including preventive screenings, prenatal care, reproductive health management, family planning, menopause management, and treatment of gynecological conditions.

We strive to provide a supportive and comfortable environment, where women can receive personalized care and access the resources they need to maintain their health and well-being. Our specialized clinics at our hospital are designed to provide comprehensive and focused care for specific medical conditions. With a team of expert healthcare professionals, advanced diagnostic technologies, and personalized treatment plans, we strive to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients. Trust our specialized clinics to deliver exceptional care, tailored to your unique needs, and support you on your journey towards improved health and well-being.
